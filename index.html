TIMELINE

College Hive started small with a goal to provide streamlined, exam-focused notes to students. Through consistent effort, we have expanded subject coverage and aim to continue improving the platform.

College Hive started as an idea to help students access organized and concise notes to help them in their studies. We noticed many students struggling to compile good notes across various subjects.

So we set out to build a platform that can deliver quality, syllabus-focused notes to students when they need it most - like during exam prep. We want to make studying simpler for students.

  • Phase 1 : July - Sep 2023

    Started Operating the College Hive Platform. Successfully captured the initial market.

  • Phase 2 : Sep - Dec 2023

    Focus on the Quality of the Notes and the User Experience System. Provided End Semester Notes Targeting Specific Branch.

  • Phase 3 : Jan - Feb 2024

    Team Building and Expansion of the Platform for Scaling.

  • Phase 4 : Mar - May 2024

    Aims of Phase 4 are to expand the Platform to All the Branches Students of BBA.
